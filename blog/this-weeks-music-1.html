    A note before the post

    Bit of an aside here before I start the post. I don’t really have strong goals for where I want the blog as a whole to go. It’ll likely be a collection of thoughts on whatever I’m interested in at the time and while it isn’t always going to be about music, this week is.

    This Weeks Music
    Album: bbpue - bbpue

    This is a pretty cool album that I ran into while clicking through related artists. The first song has a vibe thats very much “kol” so it captured my attention immediately. Its hard to really place the genre of it, but I would maybe label the album as indie electronic? Honestly it’s hard to say, but I guess thats why labeling things isn’t always great.

    I thoroughly enjoyed this album, and have found myself playing it on repeat for most of the week. Its catchy and familiar, but still fairly unique. At times the album reminds me of Alex G and LCD Soundsystem but It still stands on its own. Its production is simple and raw, but in that rawness there is beauty. More than anything i’ve heard in recent times, it has inspired me to make music again.


    Side note: After enjoying the album so much, I started looking into the artist and found this on their instagram:

    All songs produced on @bandlab for iphone and recorded on apple earbuds

    I will say I am shocked by both the production quality and the mixing of the album for something produced by a small artist on an iphone.

    Recommended tracks: My favorite jeans, Stacy, I ate myself, Stapleface
